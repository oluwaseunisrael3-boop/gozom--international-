Fire for the Day 🔥

Daily devotion to strengthen your walk with God

Burning for God

Scripture: Romans 12:11

"Never be lacking in zeal, but keep your spiritual fervor, serving the Lord."

God is raising a generation that refuses spiritual lukewarmness. The fire of God must burn continually on the altar of your heart. A life that burns for God is a life that carries divine authority and spiritual influence.

Ask the Lord today to ignite fresh hunger and passion for His presence.

Prayer

Lord, ignite my heart with fresh fire. Let my life burn with passion for Your presence and purpose.

About GOZOM International Ministries

GOZOM International Ministries is an apostolic movement dedicated to raising believers who burn with passion for Jesus Christ and walk in the power of the Holy Spirit.

Through discipleship, prophetic teaching, prayer, and revival gatherings, the ministry equips believers to live a life of intimacy with God.

Founder

Apostle Oluwaseun Israel is a revivalist and teacher of the Word with a passion to raise a generation that burns for God.
